Your Drum Tutor

Meet Troy

Troy is a professional drummer and educator based in Silverdale, NSW, with over 30 years of performance experience and 20+ years of teaching. He brings real industry experience into every lesson, helping students build strong, practical musicianship from the ground up.

He has performed extensively across Sydney in professional cover bands, as well as owning and managing his own bands, and teaches students of all ages and levels from beginners through to advanced drummers.

Troy is also highly regarded for Year 12 HSC Music preparation, with consistent Band 6 results. Alongside teaching and performing, he has worked as a sound engineer for international drum clinics featuring artists such as Kenny Arnoff, Simon Phillips, John Blackwell, and Dennis Chambers. He is a Yamaha and PAISTE endorsed artist (since 2012).

    Lessons include:

  • Private 1-on-1 drum tuition tailored to you
  • Focus on timing, technique, and musicality
  • Real-world industry experience integrated into teaching

What We Offer

Drum Lessons

Structured programs designed to take you from where you are to where you want to be.

01

Beginner Drumming

Start your drumming journey with confidence. Learn the fundamentals of stick technique, basic beats, and how to set up at the kit properly — perfect for complete beginners of all ages.

  • Basic drum kit orientation
  • Fundamental stick technique
  • Simple grooves and patterns
  • Building coordination and confidence
Most Popular
02

Intermediate Technique

Take your playing to the next level. Dive into advanced fills, rudiments, and more complex grooves that will make you a versatile and expressive drummer.

  • Advanced rudiments and fills
  • Dynamic control and expression
  • Playing to backing tracks
  • Genre exploration — rock, funk, jazz
03

Advanced Performance

For drummers ready to perform at their best. Develop the stamina, creativity, and mastery needed for live performance and recording situations.

  • Performance preparation and endurance
  • Complex time signatures and polyrhythms
  • Improvisational and creative skills
  • Studio and live recording techniques
04

Rhythm & Timing Mastery

The foundation of great drumming. This focused program hones your internal clock, groove feel, and musical awareness — what separates good drummers from great ones.

  • Metronome and click track training
  • Subdivision and feel development
  • Groove and pocket playing
  • Musical listening and awareness
